This milestone is a testament to the hard work and dedication of all the faculty, staff, and students who have contributed to the growth and success of our department.

As we look back on the past decade, we are proud of the achievements and accomplishments that our department has made. The articles, artwork, and projects featured in this magazine highlight the achievements and accomplishments of our faculty, staff, and students over the past decade. They also showcase our commitment to fostering creativity, critical thinking, and problem-solving skills in our students.
